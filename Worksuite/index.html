PHASE 2 - EARLY Q1 2026
✨ Key Improvements in Phase 2
• Full Employee US and Canada Tax Onboarding in NEO: Complete I-9 and state/provincial form processing directly in NEO platform
• Built in US and Canada Payroll Solution: NEO payroll partner handles all backend processing (replacing Gusto)
• Employee payments handled by NEO: Direct salary disbursements through NEO platform
• Integrated Timesheets: Employees enter time directly in NEO system
• In-System Request Management: All change requests and approvals handled within NEO
• Elimination of Email Dependencies: All processes move from email to automated workflows
